Input:There are 49 dogs signed up to compete in the ...

It would take 42 dogs to complete the race

list_alt Step by Step Solution

To find out the number of dogs competing, you first have to subtract 36 from 49 and then divide the resultant answer by 2.

That is 13 divided by 2 to get 6.5. 6.5 is the number of big dogs that signed up. No, this ain’t the final answer yet. The next step is to add 6.5 to 36.

The answer thus is 42.5, and since we all know that a half-dog can’t participate, so it is a hypothetical question and we have to assume 42.5 as the answer.
